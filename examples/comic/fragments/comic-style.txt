**Style:** Illustrated in the artistic style of 1960s pop-art comic books. Bold lines, ben-day dots, and vibrant colors.
**Composition:** Cinematic framing with dynamic angles.
**Lighting:** Hard lighting with strong shadows, typical of noir comics. High contrast.
**Color Palette:** Warm mid-century modern colors (mustard yellow, teal, burnt orange).

**Technical Instructions:**
1. **Object Scale:** Maintain realistic relative scale between characters and environment.
2. **Text:** Render speech bubbles clearly with legible, bold, sans-serif font in all caps.
3. **Integration:** Seamlessly blend characters into the scene using consistent lighting and shadows. Ensure characters are grounded in the environment.
